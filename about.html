About Gynaesurg

Dr. Minu Pandey Shah practicing Gynaecologist since 21 years. Her field of interest is Adolescent Gynaecology, Laparoscopy, Infertility treatment, maternity services.Over the past 20 years Dr. Nimesh Shah is dedicated to provide a wide range of surgeries through advanced laparoscopic techniques. Dr. Nimesh Shah, a highly trained surgeon with 20 years of operative experience, has dedicated his time and efforts towards furthering the scope of laparoscopic surgery in private practice and patient care. Over the last 20 years he has developed a clinical practice dedicated to Minimally Invasive Surgery and Endoscopy.Consultant Dr. Nimesh Shah firmly believes that the benefits of laparoscopic surgery should be passed on to one and all irrespective of the socioeconomic status. He thus strives to make his services and laparoscopic expertise available to patients from all strata of the society and from all parts of the country and world.
